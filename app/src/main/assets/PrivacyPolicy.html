PRIVACY POLICY - Try and Remove

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Try and Remove (“Application”) for mobile devices that was created by Markus Pöschl. The Application is giving you the opportunity to record app installations on your phone and remove them afterwards all together with a few clicks.

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information

The Application obtains the information you provide when you download the application from Google Play or and alternative deployment platform.

Automatically Collected Information

In addition, the Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile device's unique device ID, your mobile operating system and information about the way you use the Application.

Yes. I will share your information with third parties only in the ways that are described in this privacy statement.

Collected Analytics Data

The Application uses the crashlytics framework to track frequent crashes and development errors. So the Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the device's operating system name, operating system version, Android ID and Android Advertising ID. For more information which data crashlytics collects please go to the crashlytics privacy policy.

The collection of data through crashlytics can be opted out like described in the “Opt-out” below.

You may use the standard uninstall processes that may be available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace or network.
